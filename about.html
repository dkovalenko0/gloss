About us

Yael levin is a goldsmith and jewelry designer, Living and creating in Tel-Aviv Israel.

B.design graduate of the jewelry design department of Shenkar college of design & engineering, class of 2014. Learned the art of gold smithing at 16 and been doing it ever since. Yael sees the human body as a great tool for exploring form and function, In her designs she strives to enrich peoples lives with jewelry that show respect and awareness to shape, matter & beauty.

Yael Levin’s jewelry, combine classic & innovative jewelry making techniques. focusing on rich design, balancing soft geometric elements with flowing lines. Unique Precious & semi-precious gemstones are personally picked by the designer. each piece is built “around” the gemstone in order to achieve high balance between form & matter - gemstone & metal. The jewelry are meant to live with - they are designed to rest perfectly on the body, as an integral part of it.
